Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These pumpkin chocolate chip cookies are for those of us who love all things pumpkin pie and chocolate. These cookies are always a big hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups milk choclate chips

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Bake at 375 for 12-14 minutes
