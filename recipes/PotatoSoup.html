Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Description

A rich broth. Baked potato toppings. Indulgent spoonfuls. What more could you want in a Loaded Potato Soup?

Ingredients

  • 1 30oz bag of frozen hashbrowns
  • 3 14oz cans of chicken broth
  • 1 can of cream on chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 tsp ground Pepper
  • 1 package cream cheese (dont use fat free)

Steps

  1. Grab crockpot
  2. Combine all ingriedients except cream cheese
  3. Cook for 6 to 8 hours on low heat
  4. Add cream cheese and keep heated until thoroughly melted
  5. Serve with cheese,sour cream, bacon bits, and green onions.
