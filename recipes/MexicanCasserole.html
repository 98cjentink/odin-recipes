Mexican Casserole

Description

This Mexican casserole is easy to make and very tasty. I often substitute with ground turkey and low-fat dairy products, and it's still delicious! Serve this casserole with chips, salsa, and green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of red enchilada sauce
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 4 tortillas
  • 1 lb of ground turkey
  • i can of refried beans
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. cook the ground turkey on a pan
  3. Mix enchilada sauce with cream of chicken
  4. Spray the casserole dish and put a little of the sauce mixture at the bottom
  5. Cut or tear your tortillas to fit and then dip them into the sauce mixture and add to the dish
  6. Put half the can of beans on top and spread out
  7. spread half the meat on top
  8. sprinkle cheese and repeat each step until you have 2 layers
  9. top with cheese
  10. Cover foil and cook for 20 minutes
  11. remove foil and cook for 5 more minutes
  12. Serve and enjoy with tortilla chips
